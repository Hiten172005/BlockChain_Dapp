📤 Submit Fraud Report

⚠️ Staking Required:

• You must stake 32 ETH when submitting a report

• Your stake will be locked for 48 hours

• If the report is approved by validators, you get your stake back plus 0.003 ETH incentive

• If disputed, you lose your stake to the system

🗳️ Validate Pending Reports

How Validation Works:

• Review the evidence on IPFS

• Vote APPROVE if fraud is genuine, DISPUTE if not

• You must stake 32 ETH per validation

• After 48 hours, majority wins

• Majority voters get stake back + 0.003 ETH incentive

• Minority voters lose their stake

⏰ Finalize Reports

Finalization Process:

• Reports can be finalized after 48 hours

• Anyone can trigger finalization

• Stakes are automatically distributed based on votes

• Winning voters share the losing voters' stakes
